About Me

A short journy of mind.

Everyone in their lifetime may be puzzled by three question. Who I am? Where am I from? Where am I going? Because of the stochastic charactor of the nature, the choices people made are much like drawed samples from all kinds of action distribution space. It is supprised that people have naturally learned how to make decision under such a uncertain world, though it is frustrated in teaching computer to do so.

The curiosity of discovering the intrinsic mechanics of how mind works and the world behaved always drive me in studing and researching in varirous of fields. I still remember when I was at the first year of gradute school, I took and listened almost 20 courses in one semester ranging from pure mathematics, physics, cosmology, economy to cs. I also spent a lot of time on grahical model, though DL seems to be more prevailing from that time.

Norbert Wiener is a great American mathmaticion. His book titled "Cybernetics or Control and Communication in te Animal and the Machine" is begining of my batchelr major. The feedback system discussed is almost everywhere. Though feedback is not enough, the real world is multi-agent. I have studied complex network in my graduate study. The worlds behaves in a fascinated way when one and another interacts with each other. Still the global property and phenomenon of tbe world such as flocking swarm can not tell much detail underneath.

Now my primary focus is studying and researching information geometry and application to DL and RL. I think the deep learning and cohomology should have something in commone which the relation between them is not clearly investigated yet. Besides I read some books on quantum mathematics. The book I am currently reading is "Geometry of Quantum States".
